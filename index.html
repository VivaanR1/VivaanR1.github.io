The BasicFundamentals of Supply and Demand

What is Demand?

Demand refers to the quantity of products or services that a consumer in a market wants to buy at a certain price. The law of demand emphasizes that as the price of a product increases, the demand for the respective product decreases; vice versa. The quantity of the goods or products that consumers want to buy depends on the price of the good

Related Information

  • An inferior good refers to a good whose demand decreases amid the an increase in the consumers' income while a normal good refers to a good whose demand increases as the consumers' income increases
